## KEY INSIGHTS FROM RESEARCH

### Content Strategy Trends for 2026
- **Bottom-of-funnel content** (pricing, comparisons, "best X for Y") converts better than top-of-funnel guides
- **Expertise-driven content** written by real humans with lived experience outperforms generic AI content in both traditional and AI search
- **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** is the new frontier — content must be structured to appear as answers in AI-powered search
- **Multi-media content** (video, infographics embedded in posts) gets more visibility across search and social
- **Unlinked brand mentions** on third-party sites are becoming more influential than traditional backlinks for AI search rankings

### Cost/Pricing Content Notes
- "How much does a website cost" has keyword difficulty of 60-75 (hard but achievable with quality content)
- "How much does SEO cost" has similar difficulty but Ahrefs data shows most SEO pros charge $500-$1,000/month
- Website design costs: $2,000-$15,000 for small business sites, $10,000-$50,000 for intermediate
- These pricing pages are among the highest-converting blog posts for agencies

### Local Content Advantage
- Geo-modified keywords have significantly lower competition
- Content like "How to [do X] in [City]" can rank on page 1 within weeks
- City-specific posts also help with Google Business Profile visibility
- Can be templatized across multiple cities for scale (Chandigarh, Toronto, etc.)
